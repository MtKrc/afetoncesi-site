About us

Important: afetoncesi.com is not an official body or emergency service. In an emergency, call 112 and follow official announcements. This site provides preparedness information only.

afetoncesi.com is an independent information platform that brings together disaster preparedness information for Türkiye in a simple, reliable way based on official sources. It operates in the public interest on a volunteer basis.

Our mission

To make official, actionable preparedness information easy to find and use in Türkiye. We summarise guidance from AFAD, MGM, e-Devlet and similar institutions in one place; we do not replace them.

What we do and do not do

  • We provide preparedness checklists, hazard guides and official source links.
  • We do not use prediction, guarantee or fear-based language; we use calm, action-oriented wording.
  • Content is published in Turkish and English (TR+EN).
