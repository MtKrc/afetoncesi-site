Assembly Point

Use AFAD’s e-Devlet service to find your neighborhood assembly point.

← General Open on e-Devlet
  1. Sign in to e-Devlet.
  2. Open the “AFAD – Assembly Point Query” service.
  3. View the result on the map and note it down.

Note: Directions may change during an event; follow on-the-ground instructions.
